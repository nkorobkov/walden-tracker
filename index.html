Will Walden Pond be closed?

When the parking lots fill, Walden Pond State Reservation closes to all incoming visitors — no walk-ins, no drop-offs — until enough people leave. This page estimates the odds from every closure announcement posted by @waldenpondstate.

How these estimates work

  • Source. All 700+ posts from the official @waldenpondstate account, classified into closure / reopening announcements and paired chronologically. If staff didn't tweet a closure, it isn't counted — so rates may be slightly higher than shown.
  • “Chance closed when you arrive” = on comparable days (same month, weekend vs weekday), the share of days the park was inside a closed window at that time. A closed window runs from the announced closure time to the matched reopening post; when no reopening post exists it's assumed to last 2h 10m (the historical median), or the rest of the day for closures after 4 pm.
  • Estimates use the 2021–2026 seasons. 2019 data starts in late August, and 2020's COVID rules capped parking at ~50%, producing 51 closure days — about triple a normal season. Both are excluded from every probability on this page; they still appear in the per-season chart, the timeline, and the event log for context.
  • Holidays count as weekends (Memorial Day, Juneteenth, July 4, Labor Day, Indigenous Peoples' Day): they fill like Saturdays.
  • What actually drives closures: heat. Weekend days over 85°F closed three times out of four; below 75°F closures are rare even on weekends. The lot (~350 spaces) typically fills between 9 and 11 am on busy days; it reopens as visitors leave, usually within ~2 hours.
  • Weather is hourly reanalysis data for the pond's coordinates from the free Open-Meteo archive (checkpoints at 9 am / noon / 3 pm; "daytime high" = max 8 am–5 pm; "dry" = under 0.1″ of rain 8 am–6 pm). It's the weather that happened — when planning ahead, pair this page with a forecast.
  • Small samples. Some month × hour cells rest on a handful of days; every figure shows its n. Treat these as base rates, not forecasts — weather on the day dominates. For live status check the DCR alerts page or the feed itself.
  • All times US Eastern (EDT). Closure times use the time stated in the post when present, else the posting time; reopening moments use the reopening post's timestamp.
